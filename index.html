Meet David McCreesh

David McCreesh

David McCreesh is a highly experienced physiotherapist with a BSc in Physiotherapy and an MSc in Sports and Exercise Medicine from Ulster University. With extensive experience working with Inter-County GAA athletes amongst others, he specializes in injury rehabilitation, sports performance, and musculoskeletal conditions.

David takes a personalized approach to treatment, focusing on pain relief, injury prevention, and restoring mobility. Whether you're recovering from an injury or looking to improve your overall health, David’s goal is to help you move better and live pain-free.
