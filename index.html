Our Philosophy

At the core of our approach is a commitment to lasting solutions—not quick fixes. The COMPLETE method focuses on long-term health by addressing the root cause of every issue, rather than simply treating symptoms.

We take the time to truly understand your story—your past injuries, current concerns, and personal goals—so we can create a treatment plan tailored specifically to you.

Through a meticulous assessment process, we break down your issues into clear, understandable terms and map out the steps needed to guide you from where you are to where you want to be. Our promise is a transformative journey toward long-lasting, thoughtless, fearless movement.

David McCreesh

David McCreesh is a highly experienced physiotherapist with a BSc in Physiotherapy and an MSc in Sports and Exercise Medicine from Ulster University. He has worked extensively with Inter-County GAA athletes, among others, and specializes in injury rehabilitation, sports performance, and musculoskeletal conditions.
